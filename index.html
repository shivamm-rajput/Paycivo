The problem

Indian SaaS billing is genuinely broken. Here's what it's costing you.

60%

Subscriptions lost to e-mandate failures

When RBI's recurring payment rules kicked in, one Indian SaaS founder watched 60% of his active Indian subscribers disappear — cards just stopped working with zero warning.

55–90%

UPI AutoPay failure rates in 2025

Users lost subscriptions they actually wanted — not because they cancelled, but because AutoPay silently failed and nobody told you or them about it.

No GST invoice = no funding

Investors now require GST returns, invoice samples, and reconciliations in due diligence. If your revenue doesn't match your tax filings, your credibility collapses — and penalties under Section 73 apply regardless.

Razorpay gives you a payment button. That's it.

No dunning. No smart retries. No customer portal. No MRR analytics. You're stitching everything together manually — and losing revenue every month you don't notice.
